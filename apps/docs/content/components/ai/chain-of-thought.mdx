`Chain of Thought` is part of the **AI chatbot family** brought from Bridge into Gremorie in Fase 5j. It composes on top of other Gremorie primitives (`@gremorie/rx-display`, `@gremorie/rx-overlays`, `@gremorie/rx-forms`, ...) and ships with full Bridge parity.

## Notes

- This primitive was migrated from Bridge into `@gremorie/rx-ai` as part of the Fase 5j chatbot batch (Bridge parity for the AI category).
- All cross-Gremorie imports use the published `@gremorie/rx-*` packages; the registry mirrors that into per-primitive source so consumers can `npx gremorie add rx-chain-of-thought` without pulling unrelated AI components.
